Real Estate

Servicorp intends ta aid corpers in the process of buying or renting a property in their place of primary assignment(PPA).

We Understand that most times corpers face a lot of problems getting accommodation, especially if they are new to a city or village.

Our plan is to solve this problem by meeting the needs of corpers depending on the kind of accommodation they want.

This could range from Buying, renting, single to shared accommodation.

Car pooling and Hailing

At servicorp, we also offer a feature that allows corpers to carpool. That Means that corpers that share the route or work at the same place can decide to put money together and rent a taxi.

At the same time, they can place request for taxis to pick them up at various locations, we have a networ of taxi drivers that can able to dispatched at request.

Loan services

We Understand that sometimes, pressing needs come up and corpers may not readily have funds to service their needs. It could be paying for accommodation, acquiring basic amenities or personal.

we offer the opportunity to take loans from the company with the aim and plan of repaying such loans at an agreed date. These loans at an agreed date. These loans can be directly deducted from their monthly corpers allowance and can be repaid in installments.

Forum

Another feature on our platform is the forum. The forum is a room where different issues are raised and discussed by the corpers from all over Nigeria. It is imperative to have like-minded individuals share ideas And opportunities.

The forum is to foster relationships, team building and connections. we expect our registered corpers to use the forum to share job opportunities, build their bussinesses and find bussiness partners. Their would be useful for people in the same camp.

Marketplace

Corpers need to buy and sell among themselves and the best way to do this is to give them a feature where they can buy and sell. Servicorp offers this feature and we hope that passing out corpers and new corpers can trade using this feature.

They can sell anything on the platform.

Jobs and PPA

Its quite difficult to find a PPA or even retain one after the end of a service year. we intend to solve that problem by assisting in the sourcing for available jobs for our corpers.Of course, we can't make any promises but we will be able to help a few people combat the problem.

Profile And DM

We will offer our users the opportunity to register, create profiles, upload adverts, sign in and contact each other on our platform. It will save time and for people that don't feel comfortable sharing phone Numbers, they can communicate by direct messaging each other.
